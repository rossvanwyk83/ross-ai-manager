**Technical SEO Requirements:**

### Writing Style Guidelines:
- **Tone:** Authoritative but approachable (not legal-ese)
- **POV:** Second person ("you", "your shop")

- **Urgency:** Present but not alarmist
- **Evidence:** Always cite sources and provide proof
